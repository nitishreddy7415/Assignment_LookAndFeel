MIAMI

Miami (/maɪˈæmi/), officially the City of Miami, is a coastal metropolis located in Miami-Dade County in southeastern Florida (United States). With a population of 442,241 as of the 2020 census,[10] it is the second-most populous city in Florida, eleventh-most populous city in the Southeast, and 44th-most populous city in the United States.
